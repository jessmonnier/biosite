Welcome to this Biosite about Ryan Monnier!

This website was created by Jess Monnier for a Bellevue University CSD-340 project. The assignment is to create a biographical website about a person you can interview and obtain pictures of, so I took a bit of an easy route and chose my husband. Don't worry! I promise he's interesting!

The header image is of Ryan at the top of the Four Mile Trail at Yosemite National Park, a very steep climb that he motivated us through despite my gripes worries about whether we'd finish in a reasonable time to get back to our dog. (We did it!)
